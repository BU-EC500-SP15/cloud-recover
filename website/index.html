Keep your business running in the event of a failure with ReClo

Key Features

1 - Lightweight Windows Application

Allows user to initiate the backup process and establish a VPN connection to backups in the event of failure.

2 - Amazon Web Services

Our server manages a database of user accounts, backups, and recovered instances in AWS.

3 - VPN Connection

VPN connection between the client and the restored server in AWS.

ReClo is for small to medium sized businesses.

If you are a a non-technical business owner our product can help you keep your business intact without having to worry about calling IT support.
